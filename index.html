Meet I.M. Pei

1917-2019

Ieoh Ming Pei

Ieoh Ming Pei (aka I.M. Pei) was a master, world-renowned Chinese-American architect who was known for shaping the modern architecture world as we all know it today. His work has been described as large, and elegantly designed.

“If there’s one thing I know I didn’t do wrong, it’s the Louvre."


One of his most famous commissions was the Louvre Pyramid in Paris, a large and magnificent glass and metal structure. Pei states, “If there’s one thing I know I didn’t do wrong, it’s the Louvre." When he received his Pritzker Prize in 1983, the jury citation stated that he "has given this century some of its most beautiful interior spaces and exterior forms."
